Eligibility Criteria

Age Limit :

  • LKG – Minimum Age: 3 years 5 months – Maximum Age: 5 years [ 01.01.2019 to 01.06.2017 ]
  • UKG – Minimum Age: 4 years 5 months – Maximum Age: 6 years [ 01.01.2019 to 01.06.2017 ]
  • 1st Std – Minimum Age: 5 year 5 months – Maximum Age: 7 years [ 01.01.2017 to 01.06.2015 ]

Documents Required for Admission

For all Classes :

  1. Student Aadhar – 1 copy.
  2. Student passport size photos - 3 copies
  3. Mother and Father Aadhar – 1 copy each
  4. Family photo - 1 copy.

For LKG, UKG, 1st Std :

  1. Original Birth Certificate

2nd Std – 10th Std :

  1. Student Income and Caste Certificate - 1 copy
  2. Father / Mother Income and Caste Certificate - 1 copy
  3. Student Bank Passbook - 1 copy
  4. Previous Class T.C.

Admission Process

1. Entrance Exams

  • LKG, UKG ,1st Std: Oral Test Only
  • 2nd class onwards: Written Test

Question Paper Pattern :

  • Short answer questions based on previous class syllabus
  • 6 subjects each subject 10 marks
  • Total - 60 marks
  • Duration - 1 hour

2. Selection

  • Selection based on performance in oral / written exam
  • If eligible, applicant is admitted to same class else he/she is advised to join one class below

3. Documents Submission & Fee Payment

  • Submit all the documents listed above along with application
  • Minimum 60% fees to be paid at the time of admission
  • Balance should be cleared within 3 months from the Date of Admission

4. Briefing by Principal / Admin

5. Books Issued to Student
